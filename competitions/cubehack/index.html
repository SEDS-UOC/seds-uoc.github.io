Task

Your task is to design a cube satellite at a high level. You may use the knowledge you gained from SEDS SL Cube Satellite Workshop series or you may refer to any other sources to assist you in this task. All the teams should come up with their own satellite proposal with justifications for using the components used.

Rules and Regulations

Team formation

  • A team must consist of 2 to 5 members. The members have to be from the same university/Institute.
  • A team cannot change the members after the initial registration for the contest.
  • One contestant can not be included in 2 or more teams.

Task Submission

  • Every team should submit two items.
    1. A report on your cube satellite design (use the template given)
    2. A demonstration of the proposed design.
  • The report submission should be a PDF file, formatted to the template given following the structure provided.
  • The demonstration can be a CAD design, a 3D model, a simulation video or a pencil sketch.
  • When you are submitting images of your CAD design, sketch or 3D model, submit them as a PDF document with relevant captions.
  • If you’re submitting a simulation video, upload it to YoutTube or Google Drive and add the link to a text file and submit.
  • Add all the documents (report and the demonstration) into a ZIP file and upload to the submission link.

Content

  • Report should not exceed 10 pages.
  • A report should present, as completely as possible, the original work of the authors in planning the investigation, performing the work, interpreting the results, and preparing the report. Submitting or presenting others' work as your own will result in removal from the contest.
  • By submitting the report, you grant SEDS Sri Lanka, a worldwide, irrevocable, non-exclusive, royalty free license to publish or distribute the Work, to enter into arrangements with others to publish or distribute the Work, and to archive the Work.
